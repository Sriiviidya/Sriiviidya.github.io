Home Food

INNOVATION | DESIGN THINKING | MATERIAL DESIGN

Concept creation : March 2019
Role : UI Design, Design Thinking, Strategy

TASK

Find a problem in any potential or existing market that needs immediate innovation. Propose a solution for it.

PROBLEM

Students living away from home in hostels and university housings often rely on restaurants or families of friends to get access to homemade food when they get homesick.

SOLUTION

Homefood is designed to create a mutually beneficial ecosystem between students living away from home and homemakers living in the same location. It gives students easy access to homemade food and a homelike environment while creating employment opportunities for homemakers.

Why Homefood.?

13 million women have exited the labor market in the past six years. Social norms, rising family prosperity, and a lack of good opportunities are some of the reasons for this.

53% of the total drop - the largest chunk - happened among women aged 15-24.

Homefood combines the skills of Janu and the needs of Ram to give birth to a business possibility. It gives students a cheap alternative to other restaurants while also providing a source of income to unemployed homemakers.

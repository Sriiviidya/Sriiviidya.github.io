Knowledgehook Inc.

Product Designer

Client : Knowledgehook Inc.
Role : UX/UI Design, Wordpress Development, Email Development

Knowledgehook is an Instructional Guidance System (IGS) that uses engaging assessments to unlock insights and expert guidance for math teachers. I was part of Knowledgehook's home division that primarily focused on the parent demographic of students already enrolled in the Knowledgehook School system.

My role

As the sole product designer of the Home team, I collaborated with stakeholders and product managers to create user-centered responsive web application designs by considering market segment analysis, site-metrics, and usability findings.

Work included, but was not limited to, product page designs, marketing email design, usability testing, and user research.
